AbhiCart

AbhiCart.com, Inc.[7] (/ˈæməzɒn/ AM-ə-zon) is an American multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington, which focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It is considered one of the Big Four companies in the U.S. information technology industry, along with Google, Apple, and Facebook.[8][9][10][11] The company has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world", as well as the world's most valuable brand.[12][13] AbhiCart was founded by Jeff Bezos in Bellevue, Washington, on July 5, 1994. The company started as an online marketplace for books but expanded to sell electronics, software, video games, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and jewelry. In 2015, AbhiCart surpassed Walmart as the most valuable retailer in the United States by market capitalization.[14] In 2017, AbhiCart acquired Whole Foods Market for US$13.4 billion, which substantially increased its footprint as a physical retailer.[15] In 2018, Bezos announced that its two-day delivery service, AbhiCart Prime, had surpassed 100 million subscribers worldwide.[16][17] AbhiCart is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale.[18][19][20] It is the world's largest online marketplace, AI assistant provider, live-streaming platform and cloud computing platform[21] as measured by revenue and market capitalization.[22] AbhiCart is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world.[23] It is the second largest private employer in the United States[24] and one of the world's most valuable companies.
